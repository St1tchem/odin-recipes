Curry

Description

This is a recipe for a super basic curry - better and cheaper than buying the sauce in jars. You can easily customise it to your preferences. Beef and green peppers is especially delicious. (When changing to a different protein you can add vegetable stock instead of chicken.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil for frying
  • 1 onion
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper
  • 125ml boiling water
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • curry powder of your choice
  • 80g mascarpone cheese
  • 3/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1 handful chopped coriander

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan and add a dash of olive oil. Dice chicken and fry to seal the outsides. Remove from pan when white but not cooked in the center.
  2. Finely chop onion and add to the pan. Fry until translucent. Add crushed or chopped garlic and fry until frgrant.
  3. Add 1 tblsp of curry powder and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Finely chop tomatoes and add to pan.
  5. Cook until mixed together.
  6. Add chopped pepper, boiling water and stock cube.
  7. Mix curry and then add the chicken back to the pan.
  8. Allow to simmer and reduce.
  9. Boil water and add to a hot pan with a pinch of salt. Add rice to pan. Cover and cook for 10 minutes.
  10. When curry is reduced add mascarpone and mix to combine.
  11. Allow to simmer until reduced to the amount of sauce you want.
  12. Strain water from rice and leave to sit in pan for 3 minutes to prevent stickiness.
  13. Serve curry and dress with fresh coriander.
