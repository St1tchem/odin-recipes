Lasagne

Description

This fabulous lasagne recipe will become a favourite comfort food in your house for sure. Who can resist a rich tomato base filled with fresh herbs. Combine that with a well seasoned creamy sauce and you have heaven on a plate.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium onions
  • 250g fresh minced beef
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • 2 tins tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 handful fresh basil
  • 8 sheets of fresh pasta
  • 500ml full fat milk
  • 50g plain flour plus 1 tblsp
  • 50g butter
  • 200g cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Chop 1 onion. Gently heat milk in a saucepan and add salt, pepper and the chopped onion. Leave to infuse.
  2. Chop the second onion. Add a dash of oil to a pan and fry onion until translucent. Crush garlic and add to onions. Fry until fragrant.
  3. Add mince and salt to the onions and cook until the mince is browned.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of flour to mince. Stir until mixed.
  5. Add tomatoes to mince. Stir until mixed.
  6. Chop red pepper and add to mince with gently chopped basil
  7. Strain milk to remove onions and set aside.
  8. Heat butter in a pan until melted. Add 50g flour and mix until well combined.
  9. Add milk to mixture a little at a time. Mix until completely smooth between each addition. Not mixing well will result in a lumpy sauce.
  10. Add 100g cheese to sauce and mix until melted.
  11. Place 1/3 mince into dish. Layer 2 pasta sheets on top. Repeat twice more.
  12. Add half of bechemel sauce to dish. Layer last 2 pasta sheets on top. Add the remaining sauce and top with grated cheese.
  13. Bake until cheese is melted and a little brown on top.
  14. Remove from oven and proceed to stuff your face.
